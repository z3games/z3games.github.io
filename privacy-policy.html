Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Z3Games ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile games and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Account information (username, email address)
  • Profile information
  • Payment information for in-app purchases
  • Communications with our support team

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Game play data and statistics
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our games and services
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Analytics partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Third-Party Services and Data Collection

Our mobile games use several third-party services to provide ads, analytics, and game features. These services may collect and process your data as described below. We request your consent before collecting or sharing data with these providers.

5.1. Google AdMob (including Unity Mediation)

  • Data Collected: Device information (including device ID, advertising ID), IP address, app usage data, location (approximate), ad interactions, crash logs.
  • Purpose: To serve and personalize ads, measure ad performance, detect and prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.
  • User Rights: You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings (see Google Ad Settings).
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2. Firebase Remote Config

  • Data Collected: Device information, app version, usage data, and configuration fetches.
  • Purpose: To remotely configure and update game features and settings for a better user experience.
  • User Rights: No personal data is used for marketing. You may restrict data sharing via your device or app settings.
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5.3. Firebase Analytics / Google Analytics for Firebase

  • Data Collected: Device information, app usage statistics, events, IP address, device ID, advertising ID, crash logs.
  • Purpose: To analyze game usage, improve features, monitor performance, and detect crashes or bugs.
  • User Rights: You can opt out of analytics collection in your device settings or by contacting us. See Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5.4. GameAnalytics

  • Data Collected: Device information (model, OS, device ID), IP address, in-game events, session data, usage statistics, and crash logs.
  • Purpose: To provide detailed analytics on game performance, player behavior, and to improve gameplay experience.
  • User Rights: You may opt out of analytics by contacting us or adjusting your device settings.
  • Privacy Policy: GameAnalytics Privacy Policy

5.5. Consent and User Rights

We request your consent before collecting or sharing data with third-party providers. You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your device or app settings, or by contacting us. You have the right to opt out of personalized ads and analytics as described above.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe we have inadvertently collected such information should contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through our games or website.
